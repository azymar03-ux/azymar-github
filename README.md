## 🎮 The Studio

A small studio with an obsession for unforgettable experiences. Every pixel intentional. Every moment earned.

Azymar Studio is an independent studio founded on one belief: **great games demand obsession**. We're a team of designers, developers, and storytellers who refuse to ship anything we wouldn't play ourselves.

> Every project starts with a question — **what feeling do we want to leave behind?** — and everything follows from that.
